ASA DataFest 2026 — Patient Length-of-Stay Prediction

5-person team (assembled & led)

  • Predicted patient length of stay on Stormont Vail Health data — 7.6M encounters, 950K patients, ~230M total data points
  • Engineered custom "Journey Depth" feature capturing diagnosis recurrence; joined multiple tables via DepartmentKey and DiagnosisKey
  • Compared linear regression, random forest (200 trees), and gradient boosting (500 trees)
  • Gradient boosting best: R² = 0.58, MAE = 46.6 hours
  • Identified admission status as top LOS determinant; neonatology highest mean LOS (234h), cardiology shortest (~13h)
Stack: Python, scikit-learn, pandas
